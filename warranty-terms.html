Fair Use Policy – BeneSupport Extended Warranty

Thank you for choosing BeneSupport. The BeneSupport Extended Warranty is designed to give you added peace of mind. It covers defects in materials or workmanship under normal, responsible use. We encourage confident, active living — and our products are built to support your daily movement.

Coverage Scope

The extended warranty does include the following:

  • Joint support in daily life, exercise, and regular sports
  • Use during non-professional athletic activities like jogging, fitness, walking, yoga, cycling
  • Functional issues related to seams, stitching, or fasteners under normal use
  • Material failure unrelated to misuse

Warranty extension must be registered within 60 days of your original purchase date.

However, the following exceptions apply and are not covered under the extended warranty:

  • Damage caused by high-heat exposure (e.g., tumble dryers, radiators, or prolonged sun baking)
  • Visible damage resulting from misuse, including:
    • Cutting, tearing, or altering the product structure
    • Improper storage in excessively humid or chemical environments
  • Loss or theft of the product
  • Unusual or extreme usage scenarios, such as intentionally overstretching or over-compressing the material
  • Products not purchased from official or authorized sales channels

    • BeneSupport may decline late or unverifiable registrations to ensure fair use of this benefit.
